# sephirotic_tree

> 10 sephirot. 22 paths. the tree of life as a luminous network of divine emanation. each sphere has a color, a planet, a number. the paths are the hebrew letters. energy flows along connections.

**Archetype:** CURVE_PLOTTER. **Distinct from** `strange_attractors` (not chaotic) and `space_filling_curves` (not space-filling).

## Adjacency
- `strange_attractors` — chaos energy as divine emanation metaphor
- `fourier_visual_synthesis` — harmonic relationships between sephirot

## Visual Targets
- 3-column tree layout: Kether at top, Malkuth at bottom
- Sephira as glowing orbs with planetary glyphs
- 22 paths as animated energy curves (bezier or catmull-rom)
- Flashing colors: sephirot flash between king/queen scale colors
- Lightning flash: zigzag path from Kether to Malkuth
- Serpent path: back-and-forth serpentine route through tree

## Technical Notes
- Standard layout coordinates (GD Kabbalah tradition)
- Path data: 22 connections between specific sephira pairs
- Flashing color pairs: king scale ↔ queen scale ↔ emperor scale
- Planetary correspondence: each sephira maps to a classical planet
